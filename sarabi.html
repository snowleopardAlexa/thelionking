The Queen of the Pride Rock

is a lioness who used to be the queen of the Pride Lands in Disney's 1994 animated feature film, The Lion King. She was Mufasa's wife (later widow) and is Simba's mother. After her mate's death and son's supposed death, Sarabi remained strong, standing up against the tyrant king Scar. After her son's return, she joins her son, Nala, the other lionesses, Timon, Pumbaa, and Rafiki in fighting against Scar and the hyenas subsequently watching her son ascend the throne he was destined to many years before.

In Swahili language Sarabi means "Queen".
